!!! [[#using]]Using

Plug-in allow with switch-button on the plug-ins panel easily hide and show "Notes Tree", "Tags" and "Attachments" panels without changing main window size. This action available in "View" menu or with hot keys.

Hot keys can change with menu '''Edit -&gt; Preferences... -&gt; Hot keys -&gt; Reading mode'''. Your changes will take effect without restarting the program.

If you don't needed the plug-in, it can be disabled with standard means without restarting the program: '''Edit -&gt; Preferences... -&gt; Plugins -&gt; Reading mode'''.
